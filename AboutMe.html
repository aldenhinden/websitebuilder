About Me

Born in The Bronx, NYC, I grew up on Vashon, a small island in the Pacific Northwest between the cities of Seattle and Tacoma. I spent my childhood in wilderness programs, my passion for the outdoors remaining indissoluble throughout my life. I love hiking, surfing, snowboarding, and all the adventures that are found in their persuit. My favorite sport is soccer, and my favorite player is Lionel Messi.


I have always had a passion for music, playing piano for many years as a child and more recently picking up the guitar. My love blues and rock the most, with my favorite musicians being Jimi Hendrix, Eric Clapton, Stevie Ray Vaughan, and John Mayer.
