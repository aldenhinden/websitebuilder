Born in The Bronx, NYC, I grew up on Vashon, a small island in the Pacific Northwest between the cities of Seattle and Tacoma. I spent my childhood in wilderness programs, my passion for the outdoors remaining indissoluble throughout my life. I love hiking, surfing, snowboarding, and all the adventures that are found in their persuit. My favorite sport is soccer, having played it my whole life, and my favorite player is Lionel Messi. I also love to cook and have recently found a passion for boxing.


I have always been passionate about music, playing piano for many years as a child and more recently picking up the guitar. My love blues and rock the most, with my favorite musicians being Jimi Hendrix, Eric Clapton, Stevie Ray Vaughan, and John Mayer. I mostly play with a looper, but I am always looking to jam with other people! I have played one performance with a friend of mine, doing covers of a few songs in front of a live audience.


I have held various volunteer positions in my community and beyond, the most impactful of which I would recount being my time as an apprentice at the Vashon Wilderness program as well the three months I spent in northwest Nicaragua.
